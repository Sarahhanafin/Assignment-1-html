Project Deliverables

My deliverables for the project included thorough research, wireframing for the reports page, and creating the final design for the reports page. The research phase laid the foundation by identifying user needs and gathering insights. Using this information, I created wireframes to map out the structure, layout, and user flow of the reports page, ensuring it would meet both user and business requirements while fitting the design of the other pages of the web app, which were designed by other members in my team. Finally, I developed the polished, final design for the reports page, combining usability, aesthetics, and functionality to deliver a cohesive and user-friendly solution. These deliverables showcase my ability to carry a concept through from initial research to a fully realized design.
